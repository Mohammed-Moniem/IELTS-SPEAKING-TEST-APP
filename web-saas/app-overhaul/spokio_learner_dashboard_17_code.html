Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time? expand_more

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You will retain access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period.

How accurate is the AI grading? expand_more

Our AI is trained on thousands of official IELTS exams graded by certified examiners. In 95% of cases, our AI score is within 0.5 bands of an official examiner's score.

Do you offer discounts for students? expand_more

Yes! If you have a valid .edu email address, you can apply for a 30% discount on the yearly Pro Scholar plan. Contact our support team for more details.

What is the difference between Academic and General Training? expand_more

Spokio supports both modules. When you set up your account, you can select your target module, and the practice materials (especially Reading and Writing Task 1) will be tailored accordingly.
