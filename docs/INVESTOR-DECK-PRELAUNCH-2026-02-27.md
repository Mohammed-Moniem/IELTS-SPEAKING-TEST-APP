# Spokio Investor Deck (Pre-Launch)
Date: 2026-02-27
Stage: Pre-launch (product built, commercial launch pending)

## Slide 1 — Cover
- Spokio: AI IELTS Preparation Platform
- "One platform for speaking, writing, reading, listening, and full exam readiness"
- Founder name(s), contact, city (UAE)

## Slide 2 — Problem
- IELTS candidates use fragmented tools with weak feedback loops.
- Speaking and writing evaluation quality is inconsistent.
- Learners lack one place to practice all modules and track progress.
- Coaches/institutions need operational visibility, not just content.

## Slide 3 — Solution
- Spokio combines:
  - AI-assisted speaking evaluation
  - Writing/reading/listening workflows
  - Full exam orchestration
  - Progress analytics and study guidance
- Unified learner + admin system to run both learning and operations.

## Slide 4 — Product Status (What Exists Today)
- Mobile app + backend + web SaaS architecture implemented.
- Marketing, learner, and admin surfaces are built.
- Subscription and Stripe-ready billing paths implemented.
- Production-readiness gates were passed (typecheck/build/E2E/a11y in latest signoff).
- Current status: pre-launch commercialization, not yet scaled in market.

## Slide 5 — Why Now
- IELTS demand remains high across migration and education pathways.
- AI quality + lower infra cost make personalized prep more scalable.
- UAE/MENA ecosystem remains active in early-stage startup investing.

- Published plan structure:
  - Free: $0

  - B2C subscriptions (core)

## Slide 7 — GTM (First 12 Months)
- Phase 1 (0-3 months): controlled launch, IELTS-focused content funnel, first paid cohorts.
- Phase 2 (4-8 months): referral + partner program activation, conversion optimization.
- Phase 3 (9-12 months): team plan growth via coaches and small institutes.
- KPI focus:
  - CAC payback

  - Monthly retention/churn
  - Cost per evaluated session

## Slide 8 — Competitive Positioning

- Traditional IELTS prep: strong content, weaker personalized AI feedback loops.
- Spokio advantage:
  - End-to-end IELTS workflow in one account
  - Cross-module progress tracking
  - Operational admin layer for scale

- Equity math (priced round):

  - 1.5% equity => 3,333,333 AED post-money valuation
  - 2.0% equity => 2,500,000 AED post-money valuation

### Recommended Range (pre-launch, no revenue)
- Fair market negotiation zone: **1.0% to 2.0%** for 50,000 AED.
- Practical anchor to propose: **1.5% equity**.
- Do not include guaranteed "profit share"; use standard equity upside (or SAFE/convertible note terms).

## Slide 12 — Terms Guardrails

- Keep standard investor rights only (information + pro-rata if needed).
- Keep founder dilution capacity for future rounds.
- If using SAFE instead of priced equity:
  - Use valuation cap + discount, and avoid overly low cap that over-dilutes founders.
